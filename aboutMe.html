ABOUT ME:

I’m Jan-Marek Lappe, a digital media designer. Currently i am studying digital-media at the >University of the Arts Bremen and the >University Bremen .

I teach my self more an more in web design, video production, photography and programming like java and c++. I didn’t really achieve something big jet, like winning awards or something similar, but i’m young, got a lot motivation and i am working hard to get better.


A good design is not just something what looks good, it needs to be clearly functional and easy to use. I always try to create something with the highest level of usability and the simplest way to interact. Less is more.


Of course my thinking about design is always in progress, but at the moment, that’s the way i’m trying to go.
